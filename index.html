About

Michael Bahari is currently completing his Fast Track Coding Bootcamp at Coder Academy. Michael (b.1990) was born in Jakarta, Indonesia and raised in Sydney.He has studied Visual Communication at Billy Blue College Of Design (2011), and UX Design at General Assembly (2014).

Michael has worked as a: freelance Graphic Designer & Illustrator, Camera & IT salesman and most recently a UX/UI Designer for a start-up based in Jakarta. He has had the privilege of working with companies and organizations like Otomoto and Lentera Anak Pelangi.

Michael delights in melon-flavoured foods and is passionate about circular design. His approach to problem solving is deeply rooted in conceptual experimentation and research.

Ben & Mike's Song Suggestor
Ruby
06.3.19
For our first assignment we were tasked to create a Ruby terminal application (app) in pairs. My partner Ben and I decided to make a program that would recommend a song based on your mood or an occasion.
We looked at Spotify for our case study and observed how although there were numerous playlists to browse through, it took too many steps to discover a song that we enjoyed. We wondered if there was a way to shorten and simplify these steps. Our mission statement: “Finding a song that fits your mood or occasion takes effort. Imagine if Ben & Mike chose the music for you. With Ben & Mike’s Song Suggestor, you can listen to curated tunes in just a few clicks.”

After brainstorming possible categories, we narrowed our choices and grouped them into moods and occasions.
Mood Occasion
On top of the world Netflix & chill
Blue Dance battle
In love Fantasy board game night
Sleep deprived Cosplay party
Rage Fancy dinner party
A little high Hangin' out with Garret
The main feature of our app was to provide a song suggestion from the chosen category which included: the song name, artist and a YouTube link.
Download and try Ben & Mikes's Song Suggestor app here!
